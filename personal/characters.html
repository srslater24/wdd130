Risk of Rain 2

A Character/Modifier Guide

Not only are there many items, but also many survivors which you can play as. Some are tricky to unlock, so here's some tips!

Characters

Mercenary

Play normally and reach stage 5. Instead of travelling to the final boss, switch the teleporter and travel back to the first area, aka “looping”. After looping, play up to stage 8 and finish the teleporter event there. You’ll notice a strange dark blue portal shows up. Enter that, make your way across the floating rocks to the large obelisk, and interact with it until you disappear.

Acrid

Beef up with enough items to where you feel competent. I personally recommend stage 4 earliest. After which, find a Newt Altar on the map and interact with it to summon a blue portal. Clear the teleporter event, go into the blue portal, and immediately fall down the large pit. You’ll notice an open wall as you fall, make sure to land in there. Keep going that way until you find a purple portal, and go inside. You’ll find yourself in a strange void, next to a strange orb. Interact with it to start a mini-teleporter event. Stay in the zone and kill the enemies coming after you.
After succeeding, you’ll need to survive in the harsh void environment which will chip away your health. Quickly find the next orb (can be seen with a large beam in the sky) before you die from the corrosive environment. Get inside it’s zone and heal up before starting the next wave. The enemies will be given items, and more will be added to the pool. Rinse and repeat until you clear all the zones. If you succeed, you’ll be able to exit and unlock Acrid.
